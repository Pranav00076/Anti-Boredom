The goal of Klondike Solitaire is to move all the cards into the four foundation piles, each built up from Ace to King in the same suit.

  • Build tableau piles in descending order, alternating colors (red on black, black on red).
  • Only Kings can be placed in empty tableau spaces.
  • Flip cards from the stock pile to reveal new ones.
  • Move cards between piles following the standard solitaire rules.
  • Use the Undo button to take back your last move.

Win by building all four foundations as fast as possible!
